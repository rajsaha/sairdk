The BossIamHere App

The Benefit of using BossIamHere

At security companies, security managers monitor security guards. But who monitors the managers? Security managers have very tight schedules attending meetings and handling complaints. The BossIamHere app lets managers manage security guards at different locations and sites as well as submit reports online.

In your office, you can just log in to the web and run an IPCMS (Internet Protocol Monitoring System), and monitor all your manager's online event without any cost.

The BossIamHere is free app along with R.A.B or Guard Tour System.
